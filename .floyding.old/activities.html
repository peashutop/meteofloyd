the adventure

Please join us for a midsummer weekend of camping, hiking, exploring, drinking and dining at an abandoned airfield in Jamaica Bay.

12pm – 4pm arrival, lunch, exploration

Activities including but not limited to:
  • Historic aircraft tours in nearby Hangar B
  • Long walks down scenic abandoned runways 1-19 and 12-30
  • A half-hour stroll over the Marine Parkway Bridge to Fort Tilden and Jacob Riis beaches
  • Archery, remote-control car driving, and model airplane flying (equipment not included)
  • Tent-pitching, campsite setup, territory marking

4pm – 8pm campsite activities

  • Team sports
  • Unstructured social time
  • Surreptitious dinner preparation
  • Meditative airplane-watching as flights take off and land at JFK across the bay
  • Feats of strength and will

8pm –10pm evensong

  • Dinner (provided)
  • Night golf (miniature)
  • Exploration of forest primeval

10pm – 1am camping

  • Campfire with s'mores
  • Hot chocolate
  • Hot chocolate with peppermint schnapps
  • Silent disco dance party (optional)
